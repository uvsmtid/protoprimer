## Disambiguation

Problems with `boot_vs_start`, `app_vs_lib`, `exec_mode` (mode_cli vs mode_api):

*   `boot_env` and `start_app` have confusingly opposite correlation with `app` and `lib`

*   `app` and `lib` is not enough

    If it is a `lib`, it cannot restart.

    If it is an `app` (`boot_env`), it will restart.

    Then, what is `start_app`? It can restart, but it is also not a `mode_cli` (not an `app`).

Solution:

*   `app` vs `lib` is modeled by [entry_func][FT_25_62_13_55.entry_func.md]

    `app` and `lib` should likely be moved to `use_case`-s.

*   `boot` vs `start` is modeled by [entry_func][FT_25_62_13_55.entry_func.md]

    `boot` and `start` should likely be moved to `use_case`-s.

*   `mode_cli` vs `mode_api` is almost modeled by [CLI_compatibility][FT_62_88_55_10.CLI_compatibility.md]
